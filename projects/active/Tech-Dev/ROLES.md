# ROLES

Define the roles, responsibilities, and rotations for this space.

## Director / Lead
- **Responsibilities:** Strategy, administration, networking.
- **Rotation:** Every 2-4 months.

## Assistant Director / Co-Lead
- **Responsibilities:** Content management, tracking, assisting the Director.
- **Rotation:** Every 2-4 months.

## Manager
- **Responsibilities:** Task division, member tracking.

## Member
- **Responsibilities:** Participate in meetings, contribute to projects.
